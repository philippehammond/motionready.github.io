Philippe Hammond

Philippe joined Premier Tech Chronos in 2015 to lead digital transformation projects. Over the last 20 years, he started as a tech consultant working at Daimler, Embraer, Bombardier Aerospace, Alstom Power. During 5 years as manager of a service firm, his team has fully integrated the innovation process of the Formula One Team Renault F1, leading to an Official Partnership. As co-owner and CTO, he helped develop his start-up Borealis into the fastest-growing start-up in Canada, building a cloud software for the mining, oil and gas sectors.
